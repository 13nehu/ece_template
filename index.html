Latest information from department of ECE IIIT allahabad

Best project award for 5th Semester and 7th semester have been announced

The Department of ECE, IIITA has decided to give the BEST PROJECT AWARD for Fifth and Seventh semester Students to develop their hands-on skills along with class room study.

Jasveer Singh from ST Microelectronics visited the department. He was most intrigued to see the numerous projects made by our students.

Surendra Tadi from Freescale Semiconductor, (INDIA) visited the department and delivered lectures on Test and Verification. Such talks have always guided our young students, and we look forward to seeing him again.

Prof. Partha Ray visited the department and delivered lectures on analog simulators and other related areas. His talk was a most inspiring success both among students and faculty alike.

Dr A. K. Gupta Director, Instruments Research and Development Establishment (IRDE) visited our institute and delivered lecture on "Application of optoelectronic instruments in Defence Application"
